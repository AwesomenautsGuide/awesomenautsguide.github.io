Nauts

The playable heroes of Awesomenauts
Nauts:

Every naut is defined by their auto-attack (AA), jump, and set of abilities. In addition to these, there are a few important notes about each character that affect how you should use them.


Froggy G
Main Role: Assassin
Auto-attack (AA): A relatively weak single-target ranged auto-attack, but when upgraded, has high DPS to both other players and structures. Can be upgraded for piercing and increased range.
Jump: A high jump. When his boots utility upgrade is purchased, the jump becomes even higher.
Abilities:
  Splash Dash - A dash ability that allows Froggy to pass through other characters and deal damage. More importantly, Froggy is invincible to damage, but not CC or DOT during his dash.
  Tornado Move - Turns Froggy into a whirlwind, dealing ticks of damage to enemies contacting him on the sides. This ability pierces and deals high damage on base if you are able to maintain contact with an enemy. However, it reduces his movement speed and jump height. One of this ability's unique upgrades is RBAY (Right Back At Ya') which reflects all projectiles during the first second of this move, which counters many other nauts.
Note: bouncepads will not work normally when using this ability, only causing a very short hop.
Key Characteristics: Froggy G is a very fast and agile naut. He is great at poking enemies with his dash by going behind them and dashing through them. He has many survival options and does a great deal of burst damage by dashing through enemies and using the stun to apply tornado damage easily. With both abilities upgraded for damage, this combination is more than enough to kill an enemy naut. Froggy G is the most vulnerable during his Tornado, due to his slowness, and when his Splash Dash, his main escape, is on cooldown.


Sheriff Lonestar
Main Role: Fighter
Auto-attack (AA): A relatively weak single-target ranged auto-attack, but when upgraded, has high DPS to both other players and structures. Can be upgraded for bullets that explode on contact and additional auto-homing missiles.
Jump: A double jump. When his boots utility upgrade is purchased, a third jump is available. Each jump is the same height.
Abilities:
  Dynamite Throw - Lonestar throws three sticks of dynamite that explode on contact with an enemy or explode shortly after landing on the ground. Holding the ability makes the range of each stick increase, but also increases the spread of the dynamite. Each stick takes a different path that is marked when using the ability. The only way to ensure that all three sticks land on your target is to stand directly next to your enemy and use the ability, which is known as facemiting.
  Hyper Bull - Lonestar summons a bull that pushes other characters. The bull has limited health, but it can be upgraded. This ability is extremely versatile and can be used for both escape and fighting. To escape, simply use the bull to push enemies away from you. To use it to fight, you can either push enemies into your turrets or use the bull to pin them against a wall, rendering them immobile for a short time, also known as bullpinning. With the Techno Viking Helmet upgrade on the bull, a bullpin can deal extraordinarily high amounts of damage.
Tip: perform a small jump before using this ability to increase the likelihood that the bull will push the enemy, since they will be unable to dodge underneath the bull if the jump height is not too high.
Key Characteristics: Lonestar is a slow naut, with speed on the level of a tank. However, his small hitbox and double/triple jump make it easy to dodge abilities and juke enemies. His three jumps, if timed correctly, allow him to reach a jump height higher than most other nauts, letting him access certain areas easily. Lonestar can push very well, taking down turrets quickly with his dynamite and AA while using his bull to shield himself from turret damage. In addition, by coming behind enemies near your turret, Lonestar can initiate an advantageous teamfight by using his bull on all of them, forcing enemies to take turret damage while fighting you and your allies.


Leon Chameleon
Main Role: Assassin
Auto-attack (AA): A piercing close-range sword slash that does a decent amount of damage. Can be upgraded for lifesteal and a slowing effect. Can also be upgraded to deal significantly increased damage on every other slash and/or when striking the enemy from behind.
Jump: A high jump.
Abilities:
  Tongue Snatch - Leon spits out his tongue, dealing damage to characters in its path; if there are any enemy nauts within range of the tongue, the tongue stops at the first one it hits and pulls them to him, dealing damage and displacing them.
  Cloaking Skin - Leon becomes invisible for 8 seconds, leaving behind a clone with minimal health that attacks enemies that get within AA range. The clone can be upgraded to explode upon death, walk toward enemy towers, and have increased health. Only one clone can exist at a time. This ability can be used to push turrets effectively, using the clone to take turret damage while dealing damage.
Key Characteristics: Leon is an effective assassin, able to chase down escaping enemies easily and able to pull enemies in toward him. He deals a great amount of consistent damage through his autoattack; if Leon pulls you in or gets within AA range, it is often better to stay and fight him, juking around him with jumps and glass platforms. Turning your back to him to escape simply allows him to do more damage without retribution.
